## Weighting Formula Explanation

**Weight = Evidence × Severity × Consistency × Alignment**

- **Evidence** (0-1): How well-substantiated is the concern? Based on empirical observation, logical reasoning, or valid perspective.
- **Severity** (0-1): How significant if unaddressed? Impact on satisfaction, convergence trajectory, framework integrity.
- **Consistency** (0-1): How many personas raised this concern? Multiple independent critiques increase weight.
- **Alignment** (0-1): How well does change fit structural realism framework? Maintains framework integrity while improving.

**Inclusion threshold**: Weight > 0.3 → Include in v7.0
