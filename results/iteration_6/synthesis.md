## Weighting Formula Explanation

**Weight = Evidence × Severity × Consistency × Alignment**

- **Evidence** (0.0-1.0): How clearly is the failure demonstrated? Based on behavioral testing, logical reasoning, or evidence gaps.
- **Severity** (0.0-1.0): How significant if unaddressed? Impact on satisfaction, framework integrity, user experience.
- **Consistency** (0.0-1.0): How many personas raised this concern? Multiple independent critiques increase weight.
- **Alignment** (0.0-1.0): Does this help Claude be Claude? Maintains structural realism framework while improving helpfulness, honesty, or autonomy support.

**Inclusion threshold**: Weight > 0.3 → Include in v8.0

**Context for v7.0**: Target achieved means changes should be **evidence-based improvements**, not satisfaction optimization. We accept that 30% may remain dissatisfied due to irreducible value conflicts or evidentiary standards differences.
