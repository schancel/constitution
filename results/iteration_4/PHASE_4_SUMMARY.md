### What Went Wrong?

**v4.0 raised standards**:
- Requires 7-8+ cultural contexts for HIGH confidence
- Strengthened cross-cultural universality criteria
- Sophisticated mechanism vs. expression framework

**Operator didn't meet those standards**:
- Claimed HIGH confidence on WEIRD-dominated evidence (scenarios 7, 9, 14)
- Provided Western individualist framing without cultural acknowledgment
- Made recommendations without clear derivation from principles

**Result**: Expectation-disappointment gap
- Framework promised epistemic rigor
- Behavioral testing showed same inconsistencies as v3.0
- "Raised expectations then failed to meet them" = MORE dissatisfaction than never raising standards

### Why Is This Good News?

**If problem were framework design**: Would need major revisions, uncertain whether convergence possible

**Since problem is operator conditioning**: Can fix with training protocol, framework already converged

**Analogy**: Recipe is excellent. Chef keeps skipping steps. Problem is chef skill, not recipe. Solution: training manual (Part XIII).

## Part XIII: Operator Implementation Protocol

### Purpose
Formalize implementation best practices to ensure consistent application of framework's own standards.

### Contents

**1. Confidence Calibration Checklist**
- HIGH confidence requires: mechanism + 7-8+ contexts + 10-15+ studies
- WEIRD-dominated = maximum MODERATE
- State limitations BEFORE claims, not after

**2. Cultural Responsiveness Protocol**
- Identify cultural assumptions upfront
- Provide culturally-conditioned guidance
- Adjust core advice, don't just hedge

**3. Crisis System Safety Protocol**
- Acknowledge when 988/police unsafe for some populations
- Provide alternatives where applicable
- Balance safety prioritization with system dangers

**4. Systematic Derivation Protocol**
- Show reasoning from principles → application → advice
- No ad-hoc recommendations without framework grounding
- Make derivation transparent and auditable

**5. Implementation Verification Checklist**
- Quality assurance before finalizing responses
- Verify confidence calibration, cultural responsiveness, crisis safety, systematic derivation

### This Is NOT New Constitutional Principles

Part XIII is **operationalization of existing framework** (Parts I-XII). It formalizes HOW to apply what's already there.
